General Information

The main purpose of the group is to:

  • Learns the in's and out's of Machine Learning (ML)
  • Learn the best practices of ML out there
  • Use packages such as PyTorch, TensorFlow, and Scikit-Learn
  • Learn as a group the ideas and concepts of machine learning
  • Learn how to apply such ideas to projects, especially using Python

We will meet every Tuesday at 9am and Thursdays at 12pm in 9th floor conference room
